Aviation (Re)Insurance

Acton Jets Ltd is an officially approved Insurance Introducer for one of the largest Worldwide Insurance Groups specialised in the Aviation Industry.

We offer our introducer insurance services mostly to non-UK based clients.


We can get you Insurance products tailored to your Aviation Insurance needs and able to cover any aviation risks anywhere in the world (except sanctioned countries).


Aviation is one of the most crucial and valuable industries in the world today. Businesses and organisations operating in the aviation sector face safety and security concerns unlike any other. Rising costs of fuel, labour and repairs are driving higher value claims in an ever-changing risk landscape.


Even if Acton Jets is not an insurance broker itself, we do work directly with approved and experienced specialists and insurance brokers who can arrange aircraft and aviation insurance for the full range of risks from single engine private aircraft through to airfields and airports. They are also able to cover the associated businesses who may find it difficult to source insurance due to their work being airside or due to the safety critical nature of the products they produce.


The Insurance providers network we work with include groups linked to Lloyd's of London Insurance Group who are able to cover big risks and high profile clients. Lloyd's of London Insurance Group is based in the UK and is the world's leading insurance market providing specialist insurance services to businesses in over 200 countries and territories.


Our Insurance providers have a huge appetite for risks in the aviation industry. They can provide insurance or reinsurance services for:

• Private jets

• Commercial airlines

• Military Aircrafts

• Cargo operators

• Helicopters

• Aerospace manufacturers

• Airports

• Airfields

The Insurers we work with can provide coverage for the full spectrum of insurances including, but not limited to:

• Airline Hull (including Spares and Equipment)

• Legal Liability to Third Parties arising out of the aircraft operations covering Bodily Injury, Property Damage, Passenger, Passenger Baggage and Personal Articles Legal Liability, Medical and Related Expenses (including the Pilot and Operational Crew of the Aircraft)

• Personal Accident Reinsurance

• Crew Insurance: Personal Accident Insurance covering Pilots & Flight Crew

• Aircraft hull insurance

• Airside liability insurance

• Management liability insurance

• Cyber insurance

• Employers’ liability insurance

• Goods in transit

• Terrorism and political violence cover

• Professional indemnity insurance

• War insurance
